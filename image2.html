2.How Athletes Can Achieve Self-Discipline?



Self-discipline is an important thing for many athletes and it should be properly highlighted. Discipline may be difficult to achieve if people are allowed to make mistakes without retribution. Athletes should be able to create a level of credibility and they will be able to extend beyond their self-imposed limits. In order to establish credibility, it is important for athletes to maintain a proper standard of positive and constructive behaviors. In order to build and strengthen our sports performance, we should have a continual process. Athletes must be trustworthy, ethical and competent. Discipline requires a code of conduct, which shapes our behaviors. The code will allow us to develop a degree or self-discipline and authority, regardless of the highly competitive conditions.

Lack of self-discipline may result severe damage to personal productivity. Any breach and violation of the code of conduct should be swiftly responded. There could be harsh penalty related to any kind of violation, depending on the team. In some cases, players may not be allowed to participate in a number of important games. Coaches should be able to wisely exercise their discretionary power, so they are able to make sure that punishment can have positive results. Athletes shouldn’t directly denigrate or display total disrespect towards others. Any kind of physical confrontation should be deliberately avoided. It is important to have steady level of performance in any kind of sparring and general training sessions. During actual matches, they should avoid using deliberate excessive physical contacts, which may result in injuries to others.

If we want to achieve self-discipline



If we want to achieve self-discipline, we should have the proper level of knowledge. This should allow us to develop our own character, especially if we can identify our own weaknesses and strengths, Eventually, self-discipline will emerge and we will be able to control our own impulsive nature. Things that need to be improved can be handled more appropriately. Professional athletes are known for their humility, with a complete understanding that they are not perfect. By constantly improving themselves, they will have the indomitable spirit to motivate themselves against overwhelming odds. Mastery of positive tenets can’t be easily achieved by normal persons. It is important that we are able to strive on a daily basis. For a sports team to operate efficiently, they should be able to maintain proper discipline.

Without proper rules, a sports team activity will turn into a chaotic shambles. This is not an ideal situation to improve overall performance. Athletes should be properly dressed and they need to be ready for any kind of training session. If athletes arrive early, they shouldn’t sit around and it’s better for them to prepare their body and mind for the training session. If they arrive late, there should be a kind of punishment that can be delivered, without damaging their motivation. It is important for athletes to remain attentive to things that are being taught. Improper preparation could cause injuries and if it happens, there should be a small team that can provide immediate assistance.
